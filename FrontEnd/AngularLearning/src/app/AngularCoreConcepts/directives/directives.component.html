Types of Directives in Angular
  • Structural Directives: Modify the structure of the DOM (e.g., *ngIf, *ngFor)
  • Attribute Directives: Change appearance/behavior of an element (e.g., [ngStyle], [ngClass])
  • Component Directives: Custom components with their own template and logic
